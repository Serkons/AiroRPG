Combat styles give you additional bonuses in combat and can be changed at any time.
They have a max level and experience that can increase the level up to max level 10.
In the beginning each player has 3 styles Trickster, Guardian and Weaponmaster.
You can read about what these styles and others do here:
https://game-igame--rpg-de.translate.goog/docu/index.php?title=Spieler_Styles&_x_tr_sl=de&_x_tr_tl=en&_x_tr_hl=de&_x_tr_pto=wapp
