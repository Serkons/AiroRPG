The first battle is the beginning of your journey and the end of these tutorials.
You should now have a basic knowledge of the game and have no problems with the first rounds.
Remember that losing is not a shame, you will still be rewarded for your efforts.
Upset? Don't worry everything will go well if you did everything correctly in the tutorial.
Let's start by giving you the necessary combat commands:
It is described in detail here: https://game-igame--rpg-de.translate.goog/docu/index.php?title=Kampfinformationen_und_Befehle&_x_tr_sl=de&_x_tr_tl=en&_x_tr_hl=de&_x_tr_pto=wapp
+ 7/me normalattack monstername = Attack monsters with normal attack
+ 7/me used techname on monstername = Attack monsters with Technique
+ 7/me goes ignition/boost = Activates an ignition or a boost
+ 7/me reverts from (ignition) = End a boost
+ 7/me sings (songname) = Play a song (songname)
+ 7!used (item) on (target) = Apply an item to (target).
+ 7/me tauts (monster) = Taunting a monster
+ 7!skillname = Apply a skill.
I wish you good luck in your first fight and your first victory.
